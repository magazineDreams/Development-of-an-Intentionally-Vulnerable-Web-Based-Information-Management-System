End-of-Year Media Showcase

📅 25 April 2026 📍 Geoffrey Manton Building, Room GM101

Celebrate the academic year with MMU Media's flagship student showcase. Students from all media disciplines — journalism, film, photography, and digital design — will present their best work to peers, tutors, and invited industry guests. This is your chance to network, get feedback from professionals, and see the creative talent across the faculty. Light refreshments will be provided after the presentations.

Guest Lecture: Cybersecurity in the Modern Age

📅 10 April 2026 📍 Business School Lecture Theatre B

Join us for a guest lecture delivered by a senior security consultant from a leading UK cybersecurity firm. The talk will cover the current threat landscape, common attack vectors affecting organisations today, and how graduates can break into the industry. Students on Network Security, Computer Science, and Business Management programmes are especially encouraged to attend. A Q&A session will follow the lecture.

MMU Careers Fair 2026

📅 14 May 2026 📍 Manchester Central Convention Complex

Over 80 employers from media, technology, finance, and the public sector will be present at this year's careers fair. Bring your CV and come prepared to speak directly with recruiters about graduate roles, placement years, and internship opportunities. The university's careers service will also have a stand offering CV reviews and mock interview sign-ups on the day. Attendance is free for all enrolled MMU students.

Photography & Videography Workshop

📅 22 May 2026 📍 Media Arts Studio, Benzie Building

A hands-on practical workshop covering essential skills in photography composition and short-form video production. Led by two MMU Media alumni now working in the creative industry, the session will include live demonstrations, shooting exercises, and a group critique at the end. Equipment will be provided, but students are welcome to bring their own cameras. Places are limited to 20 participants — sign up early.

Student Union End-of-Year Social Night

📅 5 June 2026 📍 MMU Student Union, Oxford Road

Wind down the academic year with an evening of food, music, and good company at the official Student Union social event. The night will feature a DJ set, themed photo booth, and a short awards ceremony recognising student contributions to university life. All MMU students are welcome regardless of course or year group. Tickets are free but must be reserved in advance due to venue capacity.
